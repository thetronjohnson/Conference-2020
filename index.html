IC@MACE

"Share your knowledge. It’s a way to achieve immortality” -Dalai Lama XIV

International colloquium on recent trends in engineering (IC@MACE-2020) is scheduled to be conducted on 13th and 14th of March, 2020 at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering Kothamangalam. The ​ biennial conference is jointly organised by various departments of MACE in association with the Institution of Engineers (India). IC@MACE will be a wonderful platform for people working across various disciplines to express, share and enhance their ideas on recent advancements in engineering science. The conference envisages a unique opportunity for researchers from around the world to interact and listen to stalwarts in various research domains.



Speakers

Here's the list of some dynamic speakers and industrial experts
Prof. Dr. Franz Lehner
Prof. Dr. Franz Lehner
Prof. Dr. Franz Lehner is full professor in Information Systems and holds the Chair of Information Systems (Wirtschaftsinformatik) at the University of Passau. Before that, he was a professor of Business Administration and Information Management (MIS) at the Koblenz School of Corporate Management (WHU, Germany). After that, he worked for a short period as a founding president of the Danube-University at Krems.
He became a full-time professor of Information Systems in 1996 at the University of Regensburg and switched to the University of Passau by 2004. His articles have been published in distinguished international journals and he has authored and coauthored many books. His research interests cover the areas of knowledge management and e-learning, data and information management, mobile applications as well as cloud computing.
Prof. Dr. Hans Ziegler
Prof. Dr. Hans Ziegler
Prof. Dr. Hans Ziegler holds the chair of Production, Operations and Logistics Management at the School of Business, Economics and Information Systems of the University of Passau. He earned a diploma in Industrial Engineering from Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, a doctoral degree in Business Administration and a habilitation in Business Administration from the University of Paderborn. Prior to joining the University of Passau he was Professor for Business Computing and Information Systems at the University of Paderborn and Professor for Production and Operations Management at the Technical University of Darmstadt.
Prof. Dr. Ziegler's teaching interests are Operations Research, Operations Management and Logistics Management. His research focus is on the development and analysis of models and algorithms for scheduling and inventory management in supply chains. Prof. Dr. Ziegler has a long lasting research collaboration with Prof. Chandrasekharan Rajendran from the Department of Management Studies of IIT Madras. He is responsible for the collaboration between the School of Business, Economics and Information Systems of the University of Passau and the Department of Management Studies of IIT Madras, especially for student and faculty exchange, for the Joint Doctoral Degree Program and the Joint Masters Degree Program of both institutes.
