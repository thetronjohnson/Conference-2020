Submit Your Paper

IC@MACE 2020 invites original research articles on topics lying within the scope of the conference. The colloquium will have multiple tracks in various Engineering and Management disciplines.

Interested authors are requested to submit an abstract, not exceeding 500 words, to brief their research findings. Intimation of acceptance will be sent to the corresponding author through email. Authors of selected articles should submit the full paper in IEEE format (link of sample) with a limit of 5 pages.

Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings, provided that at least one of the authors register for the conference.

The colloquium also offers the option to publish selected papers in Scopus Indexed International Journals. Kindly note that the journal publication will be subject to the guidelines prescribed by the publisher.
