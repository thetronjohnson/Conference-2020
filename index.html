About MACE

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering is a pioneer institution in central Kerala established in the year 1961. The college is managed by Mar Athanasius College Association and is aided by the Government of Kerala. It is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University. MACE, as it is popularly known, has grown by leaps and bounds since. It has now six full-fledged departments apart from the auxiliary departments of Mathematics, Humanities and Science.
