Submit Your Paper

IC@MACE-2020 invites original research articles on topics lying within the scope of the conference. The colloquium will cover various disciplines in Engineering. Please follow the following steps to submit your abstract and full paper.

Step 1: Authors have to first register in Microsoft CMT (with your email id as the username) to submit the abstract.

Step 2: After creating the account, login to Microsoft CMT. International Colloquium at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering 2020 will be visible on the My Conferences tab. If the conference is not visible in the list, search for ICMACE2020 in All Conferences section. While clicking on the conference name a submissions page will open for entering the title and abstract of the paper.

Step 3: Enter the title of the paper and abstract (maximum 1000 words) in the space provided. Also select the subject area.

Step 4: If the full paper is ready in IEEE format and with a limit of 7 pages, upload the full paper in the space provided.

Step 5: Click on Submit and view the submission summary of the paper.

Submit Abstract / FUll Paper here

Intimation of acceptance will be sent to the corresponding author through email on or before 3rd February, 2020. Authors of selected articles, and those who have not submitted the full paper before, should submit the full paper in IEEE format with a limit of 7 pages. Authors of selected articles are requested to register for the conference by paying fees as given in the website. Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings, provided that at least one of the authors registers for the conference. The colloquium also offers the option to publish selected papers in Scopus Indexed International Journals. Kindly note that the journal publication will be subject to the guidelines prescribed by the publisher.
