ICMACE

Share your knowledge. It’s a way to achieve immortality” -Dalai Lama

International colloquium on recent trends in engineering (ICMACE-2020) is scheduled to be conducted on 13th and 14th of March 2020 at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering Kothamangalam. The ​ biennial conference is jointly organised by various departments of MACE in association with the Institution of Engineers (India). IC@MACE will be a wonderful platform for people working across various disciplines to express, share and enhance their ideas on recent advancements in engineering science. The conference envisages a unique opportunity for researchers from around the world to interact and listen to stalwarts in various research domains.

About MACE

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering is a pioneer institution in central Kerala established in the year 1961. The college is managed by Mar Athanasius College Association and is aided by the Government of Kerala. It is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University. MACE, as it is popularly known, has grown by leaps and bounds since. It has now six full-fledged departments apart from the auxiliary departments of Mathematics, Humanities and Science.
